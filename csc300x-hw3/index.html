Meal plan

Welcome to On Campus Food!

Eating well on campus can be tough! Thankfully you don't have to rely on vending machine food and packed lunches. There are plenty of options both on and near campus, but my suggestions focus on Trade St. Its proximity to the Petty building as well as the diversity in dining options makes it a standout for me. I hope you're hungry!
